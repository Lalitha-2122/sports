National Sports & Games

Explore India's Top 5 Sports with Pride and Passion

Hockey

India’s national game, Hockey is a sport of speed, skill, and strategy. It has a glorious past with 8 Olympic gold medals and is deeply embedded in Indian sports history.

Cricket

Cricket is more than a sport in India — it’s an emotion. With world-class players and unforgettable World Cup wins, India dominates the global cricket stage.

Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a traditional Indian sport requiring strength, reflexes, and coordination. It's now internationally recognized with events like the Pro Kabaddi League.

Badminton

India has produced Olympic badminton stars like PV Sindhu and Saina Nehwal. The sport is now one of the most popular indoor games in the country.

Football

Football’s fan base in India is rapidly growing, with leagues like ISL promoting homegrown talent and building a stronger national team.
